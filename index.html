Publications

  • Creating questionnaires that align with ASL linguistic principles and
    cultural practices within the Deaf community
    Published in ASSETS 2020: ACM SIGACCESS Conference on Computers and Accessibility, 2019
    Our aim is to conduct research in ways that are respectful to deaf participants. In the development of a demographic questionnaire in ASL, we encountered several challenges in both technical and linguistic-cultural areas that needed to be overcome. Our experience is shared in this papertform.

  • Toward Automated Critique for Student-Created Interactive Narrative Projects
    Published in Experimental AI in Games, An AIIDE 2019 Workshop, 2019
    Automated feedback has the potential to provide significant assistance to student game creators. Here, we present a system for generating automated, critique like feedback for students creating games in the StudyCrafter platform.
